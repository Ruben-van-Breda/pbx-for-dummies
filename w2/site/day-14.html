‚úÖ Quiz ‚Äî Day 14 (10 Questions + Answers)

  1. What is UCaaS?
    • Answer: Hosted PBX + collaboration tools.
  2. What is CPaaS?
    • Answer: APIs for voice/messaging/video.
  3. How does WebRTC integrate with SIP systems?
    • Answer: Via gateways or SIP over WebSocket with ICE/TURN.
  4. Why are valid certs required for browser media?
    • Answer: Browsers require HTTPS/WSS and valid certs for devices.
  5. Role of an SBC or SBC-aaS?
    • Answer: Security, NAT traversal, topology hiding, policy.
  6. Why plan for transcoding with WebRTC?
    • Answer: WebRTC prefers Opus; SIP endpoints often G.711.
  7. Typical pitfalls enabling SIP over WebSocket?
    • Answer: CORS misconfig, WSS upgrades, certificate issues.
  8. Why might ICE servers be unreachable?
    • Answer: Corporate firewalls block STUN/TURN.
  9. When choose UCaaS over self-hosted PBX?
    • Answer: Faster rollout, managed ops, integrated collaboration.
  10. What should your architecture diagram highlight?
    • Answer: WSS, ICE/TURN, DTLS-SRTP, SBC/gateway placement.
